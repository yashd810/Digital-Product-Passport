Inside a DPP

Core passport content, presented clearly.

Product identity

Unique identifiers, model details, manufacturer information, and market placement records.

Material & substance data

Component breakdown, material origin, chemistry, substance declarations, and supporting evidence.

Environmental evidence

Carbon footprint, recycled content, lifecycle impact data, and circularity indicators.

Use, repair & end-of-life

Repair instructions, spare parts, disassembly guidance, and recycling routes.

Compliance documentation

Declarations, certificates, test reports, and regulation-specific evidence.

Governance & history

Access rules, data provenance, workflow state, version history, and audit trail records.
