What Compliance Governance Includes

The EU sustainability regulation landscape is not static — it is a fast-moving, interconnected network of regulations, delegated acts, implementing acts, and standards that interact with each other in complex ways. ESPR delegated acts are published progressively. Battery Regulation thresholds tighten on a phased schedule. REACH SVHC lists are updated twice yearly. Member states implement directives with varying national nuances.

Our compliance governance service ensures your organisation has a continuously updated, accurate view of its regulatory position — and that your DPPs, product designs, and supply chain practices reflect the current and anticipated regulatory requirements at all times.

Continuous monitoring of ESPR, Battery Regulation, REACH, CSDDD, RoHS, WEEE, and 15+ frameworks
Regulatory change alerts with impact assessment for your specific product categories
Compliance calendar with personalised deadline tracking
Quarterly regulatory briefings tailored to your portfolio
Delegated act tracking — we read and interpret every new technical regulation published
National implementation monitoring across all 27 EU member states
Compliance posture dashboard with traffic-light status per regulation per product
