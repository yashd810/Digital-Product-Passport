Our Story

Built at the Intersection of Three Worlds

ClarosDPP was founded at the intersection of three worlds that rarely speak the same language: deep regulatory expertise, world-class product engineering, and enterprise software development. Our team brings together specialists who have spent careers navigating the most complex compliance environments in Europe and beyond — from the corridors of EU policy to the factory floors of global manufacturers.

We are a deliberately diverse group, spanning twelve countries and six time zones. Our compliance and regulatory specialists have advised Fortune 500 companies, EU government bodies, and international standards organisations on sustainability regulation, REACH compliance, and ecodesign policy — giving us first-hand insight into the regulatory requirements we help you meet. Our product development veterans have launched physical products in regulated markets including medical devices, automotive components, and consumer electronics, understanding the relentless pressure of real-world regulatory deadlines.

Our software engineers have built enterprise-grade data platforms, integration layers, and evidence-backed product data systems for some of the world's largest brands. We don't just understand the regulation — we've built the technology infrastructure that makes compliance scalable, auditable, and future-proof. We write code that handles the complexity of product, material, and circularity data, not consultants who then hand a brief to a development team who don't understand the regulatory context.

What unites us is a conviction that the circular economy transition, while genuinely complex, is a strategic opportunity for companies that act intelligently and act early. The companies that invest in proper DPP infrastructure today are building a data asset, a compliance moat, and a supply chain intelligence capability that will compound in value over the next decade. At ClarosDPP, we are not simply a compliance vendor — we are a strategic partner in building products that are transparent, trustworthy, and built for tomorrow's regulatory reality.
