The Process

How ClarosDPP Works

From initial assessment to live, compliant passport — our process is structured, fast, and built to scale with your product portfolio across all applicable regulations.

1
🔍

Regulatory Assessment

We audit your product portfolio against all applicable EU sector regulations — Battery, ESPR, Toys, Construction, Detergents — identifying which mandates apply, deadlines that matter, and where compliance gaps exist.

2
🗂️

Product Data Collection

We structure and collect the product-level data required by each regulation — material composition, carbon footprint, substance information, recycled content, and performance data — via our secure platform portal.

3
🌿

Carbon & LCA Studies

ISO 14067-aligned product carbon footprint calculations are completed, covering all lifecycle stages. Results feed directly into your passport data schema, satisfying Battery Regulation Article 7 requirements.

4
🪪

Passport Issuance & Governance

Compliant DPPs are issued with unique QR codes, hosted on EU-based servers, and continuously updated as regulations evolve — so you stay compliant without manual effort as delegated acts are published.
