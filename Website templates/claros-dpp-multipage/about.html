Common questions

Straight answers.

A Digital Product Passport is a digital record linked to a product, usually through a QR code or another data carrier. It brings together identity, materials, compliance evidence, circularity information, service guidance, and end-of-life instructions.

The exact obligation depends on product category and regulation, but manufacturers, importers, brand owners, and actors placing regulated products on the market are most likely to need a passport strategy.

A PDF is static. A passport is structured, traceable, updateable, and can support versioning, access control, evidence links, QR delivery, and public-facing summaries.
