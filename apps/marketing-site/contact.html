Common Questions

How long does it take to build a DPP? ▾
For companies with reasonable data availability, we can issue first compliant DPPs within 8–12 weeks. Full portfolio coverage with material, carbon, and compliance data collection typically takes 6–18 months depending on product complexity and data readiness.
Do you work with small and mid-sized companies? ▾
Absolutely. We have service tiers designed for SMEs with a smaller number of regulated products as well as enterprise programmes for companies with hundreds of SKUs. Contact us and we'll find the right fit for your scale and budget.
What product categories are currently in scope? ▾
DPP scope is expanding through ESPR working plans, sector-specific rules, and connected sustainability data obligations. If you're unsure whether your product is in scope, we'll help map the likely requirements.
Can you integrate with our existing ERP or PLM system? ▾
Yes. Our platform provides REST APIs that integrate with SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, Siemens Teamcenter, PTC Windchill, and most other enterprise systems. We also support flat-file imports for companies without API integration capability.
