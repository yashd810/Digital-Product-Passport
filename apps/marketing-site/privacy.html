1. Who we are

ClarosDPP is a Digital Product Passport platform and compliance governance service that helps organisations structure, manage, and publish product passport information.

For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, "ClarosDPP," "we," "our," or "us" means the operator of the ClarosDPP website and related services.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the website, respond to enquiries, provide services, and improve the ClarosDPP experience.

Information you provide

  • Contact details when you request a demo, submit a form, or email us
  • Account information if you create a workspace or use the platform
  • Product data, passport content, and compliance information you upload
  • Messages, feedback, or support requests

Website and technical information

  • Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and referrer information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (where permitted)
  • Performance data to help us understand how the site is used

Platform and service information

  • Features used, workspace activity, and usage patterns
  • Device and browser information for compatibility and support

3. How we use information

We use information to run ClarosDPP responsibly and to provide a clear, secure, and useful Digital Product Passport experience.

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the website and services
  • Respond to enquiries and process demo requests
  • Send service updates, security alerts, and account notifications
  • Analyse usage to improve features, performance, and user experience
  • Meet legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse
  • With permission, send marketing communications about ClarosDPP and related services

We do not sell personal information.

4. Legal bases for processing

Where GDPR or similar data protection laws apply, we rely on one or more legal bases depending on the context.

  • Contractual necessity: Processing needed to provide services or fulfil your requests
  • Legitimate interests: Operating, improving, and securing the platform
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly agreed (for example, marketing communications)
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

5. Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for essential functionality, security, analytics, and performance measurement.

Where required, we will ask for consent before using non-essential cookies. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some parts of the website may not work as intended.

6. Marketing communications

If you request a demo, contact us, or sign up for updates, we may send relevant business communications about ClarosDPP, Digital Product Passports, compliance readiness, and related services.

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe option in an email or by contacting us at hello@claros-dpp.online.

7. How we share information

We may share information with trusted providers only when necessary to run the website, deliver services, or meet legal obligations.

  • Service providers who host, support, or analyse the platform
  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law
  • Successor organisations in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale

Service providers may only process information for agreed purposes and must protect it appropriately.

8. International transfers

Some service providers may process information in countries different from your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as contractual protections, adequacy decisions, or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

9. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, accounting, or contractual reasons.

  • Account information: retained while your account is active, then deleted or anonymised
  • Contact and demo requests: kept for business and compliance purposes
  • Marketing preferences: retained until you opt out
  • Analytics data: typically retained for up to 13 months

When information is no longer needed, we delete, anonymise, or securely archive it.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

  • Encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication protections
  • Staff training on data protection and privacy

No online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to protect information responsibly.

11. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal information.

  • Right to access the information we hold about you
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your information
  • Right to restrict how we process your information
  • Right to data portability (receive your data in a structured format)
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@claros-dpp.online. We may need to verify your identity before responding.
