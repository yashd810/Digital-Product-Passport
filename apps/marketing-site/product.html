Battery Digital Product Passport

The first hard DPP deadline is already fixed. From 18 February 2027, relevant industrial batteries and EV batteries placed on the EU market will need a compliant battery passport under Regulation (EU) 2023/1542. ClarosDPP is built to operationalise that work now rather than treat it as a last-minute formatting exercise.

Battery passport delivery is where governance matters most: carbon footprint declarations, recycled content evidence, chemistry, due diligence, and lifecycle records all need to stay connected and reviewable.
